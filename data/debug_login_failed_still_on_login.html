Season 71: the Utopia Expansion and Skyscraper League Structure

Utopia has always performed better than anyone expected, hence the improvised expansion of its 4th division, from the usual 64 leagues to the very unwieldy 96 it ended up with. We tried to give all Utopia managers the same starting circumstances, but in the process, promotion and demotion dynamics suffered (only one team could progress to division 3 per league). Once we almost filled all 96 div 4 leagues, the only solution to prevent running out of room was a reorganization, as we announced last season.

Yes, Utopia now has the usual 64 division 4 leagues, and yes, we had to demote a large number of teams to division 5, BUT, we have also limited the number of division 5 leagues to 64, which makes promotion much easier (6 teams per league!) AND we are making sure the division 5 teams have the same financial circumstances as their division 4 counterparts. Lower divisions of Utopia, should they be necessary, will also adhere to these rules (64 leagues, same circumstances for all teams).

Furthermore, inspired by the new “Skyscraper” shaped league organisation of Utopia, we’ve decided to implement the same to all countries with more than 4 divisions! Up to now, each 5th division used to consist of 256 leagues, making the league organization sort of like a pyramid, but now, they also have just 64 leagues. This will streamline many back-end processes while leaving lots of room for new signups.

Enough about the leagues, we still have to address the Garbage Time implementation! Since last season, we’ve added the option of disabling garbage time from triggering in private leagues, which passed the test, and from this season on, the same option is available in scrimmages! If the option passes muster, we will implement it for all match types next season.

On the other hand, we are working on some other adjustments affecting training, stamina, the sports psychologist and related aspects of the game to promote better minute management and discourage short rosters. More details on that will be announced in a separate news post when the changes are finalized.

Welcome to Season 64: AutoBid!

Finally, the much requested AutoBid feature is here! Bidding on both players and staff has just become much easier. The way it works is very similar to auction sites; instead of bidding a fixed amount (which you can still do - we didn’t remove the old Bid button), you can now set an amount (maximum bid) up to which you are ready to bid. The system will then bid for you just the lowest necessary amount to take over the auction (a difference of at least $1000 or 2% still applies).

Other bidders can then make bids (normal or automatic ones) to try and overbid you, but once the max bid has been overbid, even by $1, that’s it, the new bidder has taken over! In case of ties, the priority is with the earlier bid, “automatic” or “normal”. But, in the end, the highest bid wins, just as it always has.

The system checks the available amount of cash of the max bid holder with each bid, so not having enough funds to cover it will result in an overbid. Also, there are no limits on how many auto bids you can set at any given time. We believe the system is intuitive and fair and would like to encourage you to try it out.

Team consolidation: addendum and clarification

Our plan to consolidate all leagues is going forward, but since there were some concerns and ambiguity, we will try to clarify the process, as much as we can.

As an example, we should take a country with semi-filled division III. The top league will, of course, stay as it is. Division II also, as it will be filled completely by the newly promoted human controlled teams. Division III will be consolidated as follows: human teams go to leagues with lower numbers (III.1, III.2, etc...), while "fresh" teams go to highest available numbers (III.16, III.15, etc...) and bots fill the middle. There should be, if available, at least 4 bots per league in all cases to make sure there are at least a couple of easy wins for everyone.

In leagues where there's too many human controlled teams to make the consolidation work as described, placing "fresh" teams separately with 4 bots will take preference over placement of other human controlled teams.

We expect to have special cases and some possible niggles in some countries, but we will follow common sense and try to fix the placements as best we can, making changes as we go. Exciting times ahead!

Mid-season announcement: Team consolidation for all leagues!

For our Utopia users, this is nothing new and has been implemented from the start, making sure teams are grouped with as many other human controlled teams as possible. Since we already give human teams total priority in promotion (over bot teams), we decided to implement this new team placement starting this offseason!

Of course, there are some other aspects to this:
1. Top league teams won't be affected, of course.
2. Most human teams will change their current league and move laterally to new leagues of the same division - but now they will play mostly human opponents!
3. To offset possible losses of income, we will match the promotion awards for all promoted teams - no matter whether they are playoff winners or just promoted due to available empty slots!
4. Fresh and newly signed up teams will be placed in leagues populated only by bots or other such teams - to make sure they don't get overwhelmed right off the bat. We consider all teams 1 season or younger "fresh"

We hope you will enjoy this new initiative to make BB more competitive and fun!
